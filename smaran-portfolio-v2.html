How I Work

Design is a conversation
between clarity
and constraint.

Great product design starts before the screen opens. Research, systems thinking, and honest collaboration are what turn good intentions into shipped products that move numbers.

01
Research-First, Always

Every decision is grounded in user interviews and data — not assumptions. Before a single frame is made.

02
Systems Over Screens

Three design systems across three companies — each cutting handoff time by 25–60%.

03
AI as a Tool, Not a Buzzword

Designed AI UX across healthcare, fintech, and media — focused on reducing friction, not adding complexity.

04
Accessibility by Default

WCAG 2.1 compliance is a day-one design constraint for me — especially in healthcare and fintech.

05
Collaboration Without Ego

Run design sprints, workshops, and mentored junior designers. Best work happens when design is a shared language.

06
Outcomes Over Outputs

Stay involved through implementation, track real-world metrics, and iterate on what users actually do.
