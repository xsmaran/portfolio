Who I Am

A designer obsessed with details

I'm a Computer Science graduate turned self-taught product designer who believes great design goes beyond pixels on screen. It's about creating moments that make people feel something.

Over the past years, I've collaborated with teams at 2070 Health, DSP Mutual Fund, and VeeFly, working on healthcare platforms, fintech applications, and AI-driven experiences. Each project taught me something new about balancing user needs with business goals.

When I'm not designing, I'm traveling across India, exploring new cuisines, documenting these experiences, or binge-watching anime. These adventures constantly fuel my design thinking and keep my perspective fresh.
